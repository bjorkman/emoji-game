Privacy Policy

Emoji Games

1. Introduction

Emoji Games ("we", "our", "the app") is a free mobile game developed by an independent developer. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Contact: emojithegame.app@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide core game features:

  • Anonymous identifier — A random ID generated when you first open the app. This is not linked to your name, email, or Apple ID.
  • Nickname — An optional display name you choose, used on leaderboards.
  • Game scores — Your scores, completion times, and game results for leaderboard and challenge features.
  • Push notification token — If you opt in to notifications, we store a device token to send game-related notifications.

We do not collect your real name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Display your scores on global, friends, and challenge leaderboards
  • Enable the challenge and friends features
  • Send push notifications (if you opt in)

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Cloud database and anonymous authentication. Data is stored securely on Supabase's servers. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Expo / EAS — App delivery and over-the-air updates. Expo Privacy Policy

5. Advertising (Future)

We may introduce ads in a future version of the app using Google AdMob. If we do:

  • You will be asked for consent before any ad tracking occurs (via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt on iOS)
  • If you decline tracking, you will still see ads — they just won't be personalized
  • EU users will see a separate consent form for GDPR compliance (via Google's User Messaging Platform)
  • This privacy policy will be updated before ads are introduced

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Your nickname and scores are visible to other players on leaderboards. Your anonymous ID is never displayed publicly.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you use the app. Since accounts are anonymous, data cannot be automatically linked back to you if you reinstall the app.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to processing or request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, email us at emojithegame.app@gmail.com with your anonymous player ID (found in the app).

9. Children's Privacy

Emoji Games does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app uses anonymous authentication and does not require any personal details to play.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Significant changes (such as the introduction of ads) will be communicated in-app.
