# Game Category Ideas

Brainstormed ideas for new emoji guessing game categories. Target audience: kids/teens. Goal: fun, unexpected, shareable — "you gotta try this" factor.

## Pop Culture / Fandoms
- **Anime Characters** — Naruto, Demon Slayer, One Piece etc. Huge teen audience, very shareable
- **Sanrio Characters** — Hello Kitty, Cinnamoroll, My Melody. Trending hard with Gen Z/Alpha
- **Disney/Pixar Movies** — Universal recognition, works for all ages
- **Superheroes** — Marvel/DC characters from emoji clues. Broad appeal

## Sports
- **Soccer/Football Teams** — Club crests described in emoji (colors, animals, cities). Polarizing in the best way — fans will obsess
- **Olympic Sports** — Guess the sport from emoji. Accessible, surprising combos

## Internet / Gen Z Native
- **Memes** — Guess the meme from emoji. Extremely shareable, "how did they even do that"
- **TikTok Trends / Viral Moments** — Niche but kids would lose their minds
- **YouTubers/Streamers** — Guess the creator. Very engaging for the target demo

## Food & Lifestyle
- **Fast Food Items** — Guess the menu item or chain. Universal, funny, unexpected
- **Candy/Snacks** — Skittles, Pocky, Haribo. Light and fun

## Music
- **Song Titles** — Guess from emoji. Works across genres
- **Music Festivals / Concerts** — Niche but fun

## Wildcard / "Tell Your Friends"
- **Zodiac Signs** — Guess the sign from emoji traits. Teens are obsessed with astrology
- **Roblox/Minecraft/Fortnite** — Guess the game, skin, or map. Directly in the target demo's world
- **Scary Stories/Urban Legends** — Slenderman, Bloody Mary, etc. Spooky + fun
- **Emoji Pickup Lines** — Guess the cheesy line. Hilarious, very shareable

## Top Picks (highest "tell your friends" factor)
1. **Memes** — Instant viral potential
2. **Anime Characters** — Massive, passionate fanbase
3. **Fast Food** — Unexpected, everyone has opinions
4. **Soccer Teams** — Polarizing = engagement
5. **Sanrio** — Trending, aesthetic, very on-brand for the demo
