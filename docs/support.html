Emoji Games

Emoji Games is a free emoji guessing game where you decode emoji clues to guess movies, animals, countries, K-pop groups, and more. Challenge your friends and compete on global leaderboards.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?
Pick a game category from the home screen. You'll see emoji clues — type your answer and submit. You get points for each correct answer, with bonus points for speed.

How do challenges work?
After finishing a game, tap "Challenge Friends" to get a share code. Send it to a friend — they enter the code on their home screen to play the same game and compare scores.

Can I change my nickname?
Yes, tap on your nickname on the home screen to change it at any time.

I lost my scores after reinstalling. Can I get them back?
Since the app uses anonymous accounts, reinstalling creates a new account. Unfortunately, we cannot recover scores from a previous installation.

Is the app free?
Yes, Emoji Games is completely free to download and play.
